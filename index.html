Welcome to AIOU Allama Iqbal Open University

AIOU Allama Iqbal Open University Allama Iqbal Open University (AIOU) is a renowned institution in Islamabad, Pakistan, named after the great poet-philosopher Allama Muhammad Iqbal. Established in 1974, it is one of the largest universities in Pakistan by enrollment. AIOU primarily focuses on distance education, providing a platform for students who are unable to attend conventional universities due to various reasons such as work or family commitments. Offering a wide range of programs at various academic levels, including certificates, diplomas, undergraduate, and postgraduate degrees, AIOU caters to the educational needs of a diverse student body. Its flexible learning approach allows students to study at their own pace and convenience, making education accessible to a broader audience across Pakistan and beyond. AIOU is known for its commitment to promoting education and providing opportunities for lifelong learning. It emphasizes quality education, research, and innovation, aiming to empower individuals and contribute to the socioeconomic development of the nation.
